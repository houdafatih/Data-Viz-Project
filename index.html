Beyond The Box Score : Exploring NBA Player Momentum from Game to Game

Visualization 1 — Season Momentum Timeline

Read left to right: each dot is one game. Higher dots mean a stronger game for the selected metric. Orange points are hot games, blue points are cold games, and the dashed line shows the player's usual level.

Visualization 2 — Next-Game Response View

Each row compares one game with the next game. Use it to see whether the selected metric went up, down, or stayed about the same after hot, cold, or normal games.

Visualization 3 — Hot/Cold Streak Explorer

Each small square is one game. Red means above the player's baseline, blue means below it, and white means normal. The streak list shows runs of repeated hot or cold games.

Visualization 4 — Context Impact View

Each dot is a game and its next game. The x-axis shows what happened in game n, and the y-axis shows the next-game response. Separate panels let you compare contexts like home/away or win/loss.
